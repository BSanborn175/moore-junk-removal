Who We Are

Moore County Junk Removal is an independent, locally operated hauling and cleanout company focused entirely on serving Moore County, NC. We're not a 1-800 franchise that dispatches a contractor you've never heard of. When you call us, you're reaching the people who will show up at your door.

We do.

Where It Goes

How We Handle Your Items Responsibly

We don't just dump everything in the same pile. Every load is sorted at pickup so usable items stay out of the landfill.

♻️

Donated to Local Charities

Furniture, appliances, and household goods in usable condition go to the Aberdeen Habitat for Humanity ReStore and other local Moore County charitable organizations.

🌱

Composted or Mulched

Clean organic material — branches, logs, brush, and untreated wood — is taken to local compost facilities or wood processors in the Sandhills region.

🔩

Metals Recycled

Steel, aluminum, copper, and other metals from shed demolitions, hot tubs, and appliances are sorted and sent to metal recycling facilities — not the landfill.

💻

Electronics (E-Waste)

Office computers, monitors, printers, and consumer electronics follow North Carolina e-waste disposal regulations and are taken to licensed e-waste facilities.

🏭

Landfill — Last Resort

Non-donatable, non-recyclable items go to the Moore County Solid Waste Facility on Old Millstone Road in Carthage. All landfill fees are included in your quoted price.

⚠️

Hazardous Materials

We do not transport hazardous chemicals, asbestos, or biohazard waste. Paint cans with dried paint are accepted. We'll let you know upfront if something requires a specialist.
