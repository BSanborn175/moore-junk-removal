Junk Removal Across All of Aberdeen

Aberdeen sits at the crossroads of Moore County — bordered by Southern Pines to the north, Pinebluff to the southeast, and the NC 5 and NC 211 corridors connecting it to the wider Sandhills region. Its charming historic downtown, Victorian-era neighborhoods, and mix of longtime residents and newer developments make Aberdeen one of our most active service areas for estate cleanouts, furniture removal, and residential junk hauling.

We regularly serve residents throughout Aberdeen's historic district along Bethesda Road and the old Aberdeen & Rockfish Railroad corridor, homes in the neighborhoods off Saunders Street and Poplar Street, properties along the NC 5 corridor between Aberdeen and Southern Pines, and rural acreage on the outskirts of town toward Pinebluff.

Estate cleanouts are common in Aberdeen's older residential neighborhoods. We work with families, probate executors, and realtors to clear properties on real estate timelines — often coordinating donation of usable items to the Aberdeen Habitat for Humanity ReStore on US 1.
