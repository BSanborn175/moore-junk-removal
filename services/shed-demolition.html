Frequently Asked Questions

Shed Demolition FAQs

Do you remove the concrete pad or floor foundation?

Our standard pricing includes the demolition and hauling of the wooden, metal, or plastic structure itself. Demolition and removal of concrete slab foundations require heavy equipment (jackhammers, bobcats) and is priced separately as a custom quote. Let us know if you need the slab removed!

Do you need permits for backyard shed demolition?

In most Moore County municipalities (including Pinehurst and Southern Pines), permits are not required for removing detached accessory structures under a certain square footage (typically 120–200 sq ft). However, if your property is subject to strict HOA guidelines (e.g. Pinehurst or Whispering Pines), please double-check their notifications beforehand.

What do you do with the waste?

Any metals from structural posts, roofing, or siding are sorted and recycled. Scrap wood is taken to the Moore County landfill or local biomass processors. Any salvageable tools or hardware are cleaned and donated.
