Frequently Asked Questions

Commercial Hauling FAQs

Are you fully licensed and insured?

Yes. Moore County Junk Removal is fully licensed, general liability insured, and our veteran crews are covered under appropriate worker compensation protocols. We can provide Certificates of Insurance (COI) for commercial property managers upon request.

Can you coordinate after-hours pickups?

Yes. We understand that offices and retail stores must operate during the day. We offer early morning, late evening, or Saturday pickup schedules to minimize business disruption and coordinate with your facility manager.

Do you recycle old computers and monitors?

Yes. We follow strict e-waste regulations. Office computers, server systems, printers, and screens are separated from general office trash and taken to local electronic waste recycling facilities in North Carolina.
