Frequently Asked Questions

Estate Cleanout FAQs

Do I need to be present during the estate cleanout?

No, you do not need to be physically present. We frequently work with out-of-town executors, real estate agents, and attorneys. We can access the property via lockbox or key codes, provide photo updates before/after, and coordinate everything remotely.

What do you do with items that can be donated?

We believe in giving back. Usable furniture, appliances, toys, and clothing are carefully packed and taken to local Moore County thrift stores and donation networks like the Aberdeen Habitat ReStore. We provide donation receipts when requested.

How fast can you complete a full home cleanout?

Most standard residential home cleanouts can be completed within 1 to 2 days, depending on the volume of items. Our veteran-led crew operates efficiently to meet tight real estate closing deadlines.
