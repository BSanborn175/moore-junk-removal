What Is a Hoarder Cleanout?

A hoarder cleanout is the full clearing of a home or property where excessive accumulation — sometimes spanning years or decades — has made the space difficult or impossible to use normally. These situations require more than a standard junk hauling crew. They require patience, organization, discretion, and a team experienced in handling large volumes of mixed material across every room of a home.

In Moore County, we handle hoarder cleanouts for homeowners, adult children managing a parent's property, probate executors clearing inherited homes in Pinehurst and Southern Pines, landlords dealing with tenant hoarding situations, and property managers in the Sandhills region who need properties restored quickly before re-listing.

Our Approach: Respectful and Efficient

We do not rush, judge, or discard without thought. We work room by room, sorting items for donation to local Moore County charities (including the Aberdeen Habitat for Humanity ReStore), items for recycling, and items for disposal at the Moore County landfill in Carthage. Every property is left broom-clean when we finish.

Frequently Asked Questions

Hoarder Cleanout FAQs

How do you handle hoarder cleanouts in Moore County?

We approach every hoarder cleanout in Moore County with care and zero judgment. Our crew works methodically through each room, separating items for donation, recycling, and disposal. We work at the pace that is comfortable for the homeowner or family and can coordinate directly with executors and property managers without requiring the owner to be present.

How much does a hoarder cleanout cost in Moore County, NC?

Hoarder cleanout costs in Moore County typically range from $350 to $1,500+ depending on the size of the home, severity of accumulation, number of truckloads required, and whether multiple visits are needed. We provide a free on-site or photo assessment and upfront quote before any work begins — no surprise fees.

Can you clean out a hoarder house without the owner being present?

Yes. We frequently work on behalf of adult children, estate executors, probate attorneys, and property managers when the property owner is unable or unwilling to be present. We can access the property via key or lockbox and provide photo updates throughout the process.

Do you handle biohazard situations during hoarder cleanouts?

We do not handle biohazardous materials such as human waste, animal waste, or chemical hazards — those require licensed biohazard remediation companies. However, we work alongside remediation crews and can handle all non-hazardous junk removal and debris hauling once hazardous areas have been cleared.

How long does a hoarder cleanout take near Pinehurst?

A standard single-family hoarder home cleanout in Moore County typically takes 1 to 3 days depending on volume. Severe hoarding situations requiring multiple truckloads may take longer. We provide a realistic timeline during our free initial assessment.

Do you donate usable items during hoarder cleanouts?

Yes. Wherever possible, we sort and donate usable furniture, clothing, housewares, and appliances to local Moore County charities including the Aberdeen Habitat for Humanity ReStore and local thrift organizations. We provide donation receipts when requested.
