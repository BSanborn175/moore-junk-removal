Frequently Asked Questions

Cleanout FAQs

Will you climb into my attic to pull items out?

Yes! Our crew is fully insured and equipped to safely access attics (pull-down stairs or crawl hatches) and walkout basements. We handle the physical climbing, lifting, and carrying so you don't have to.

Can you haul away old cans of paint or motor oil?

Due to environmental regulations, we cannot accept wet liquid paint, oil, solvents, or chemicals. However, if you dry out the paint using kitty litter or a commercial paint hardener, we can safely haul the dried paint cans.

Do you offer garage organization services?

We specialize in debris removal and hauling. While we do not install shelving or design organization systems, we will leave you with a blank slate by clearing out all the unwanted clutter and sweeping the floor clean.
