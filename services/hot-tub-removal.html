Safe & Efficient Teardown

Dismantling Spas & Hot Tubs Safely

An old, unused hot tub isn't just an eyesore; it's a safety hazard and a breeding ground for pests. Moving one requires specialized tools, heavy lifting, and careful planning. We disconnect, cut, and remove above-ground spas and in-deck hot tubs across Pinehurst, Southern Pines, and Whispering Pines.

Important: Before we arrive, please ensure that the electrical power supply to the hot tub has been safely disconnected at the breaker by a licensed electrician, and the spa is completely drained. Once that is done, we take care of the rest!

Full Debris Cleanup

We don't just haul the main tub shell. We take the cover, the pump, steps, surround decking if requested, and all associated piping. We sweep up the patio, deck, or concrete pad so your yard is immediately ready for its next use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Hot Tub Removal FAQs

Will you disconnect the electricity and plumbing?

We require the main electrical 220v line to be disconnected by a licensed electrician before our arrival. Our team does not handle hot wiring or electrical disconnects for safety and liability reasons. We can assist with minor water hose plumbing disconnections.

Do you remove hot tubs that are sunk into a deck?

Yes, we do! In-deck spas require more precise cutting and rigging to lift the pieces out without damaging your surrounding deck structure. We have the tools and experience to handle these complex extractions safely.

What happens to the metal pumps and wiring?

All valuable metals, including copper coils, aluminum fittings, and steel pumps, are salvaged and taken to local metal recycling facilities in Moore County to ensure they stay out of the landfill.
