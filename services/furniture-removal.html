Heavylifting Done For You

Furniture Hauling & Donation Dropoff

Trying to carry a heavy sofa down narrow stairs or maneuvering a king-sized mattress out the door can be dangerous for you and can easily damage your drywall or woodwork. At Moore County Junk Removal, our team is trained in professional moving techniques to load and transport heavy items safely.

We serve homes and apartments across Pinehurst, Southern Pines, Aberdeen, and Carthage. We handles mattresses, recliners, dressers, dining sets, office furniture, patio furniture, and heavy entertainment centers.

Keeping Useful Items Out of Landfills

If your old furniture is still in decent shape (no structural damage or major stains), we will make every effort to donate it. We drop usable items at local charities, including the Aberdeen Habitat for Humanity ReStore, so they can find a new home instead of rotting in a landfill.

Frequently Asked Questions

Furniture Disposal FAQs

Will you remove a heavy couch from my second-floor apartment?

Yes. Our team is fully insured and has the equipment and strength to haul heavy couches, dressers, and mattresses down stairs and around tight corners. We handle the physical labor safely.

Can you donate my furniture and provide a donation slip?

We work to donate items in good condition to local thrift stores and charities. If the donation center provides a receipt, we will be glad to mail or email it to you. Please note that donation centers make final decisions on what items they can accept.

What do you do with mattresses?

For health and safety reasons, local thrift stores do not accept used mattresses. We dispose of them at local waste transfer stations or recycle them at designated fiber-reclamation stations when possible.
