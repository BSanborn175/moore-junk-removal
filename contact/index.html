Common Questions

Contact & Scheduling FAQs

How do I get a junk removal estimate in Moore County?

The fastest way is to text photos of your items to (910) 420-8159. We typically reply within minutes with a firm price range. You can also call directly or use the quote form above.

What are your hours?

We operate Monday through Saturday, 7AM to 7PM throughout Moore County. Same-day service is available when you reach us before noon. We are closed on Sundays.

Do I need to be home when you pick up?

For most jobs, yes — we need access to the items and your approval on what gets removed. However, for estate cleanouts and eviction cleanouts, we often work with property owners or representatives who are not present. We can coordinate via phone, email, and key/lockbox access.

How quickly do you respond to quote requests?

Text photo quotes are typically answered within 15–30 minutes during business hours. Phone calls are answered live. Form submissions are typically followed up within 2–3 hours during business hours, Monday through Saturday.

Do you serve gated Pinehurst communities like Pinewild, Forest Creek, and CCNC?

Yes. We regularly work in gated Pinehurst golf communities including Pinewild Country Club, Forest Creek, Country Club of North Carolina (CCNC), Mid Pines, Foxfire Village, and others. Let us know your community when you call or fill out the form so we can plan for gate access.

Can you provide a Certificate of Insurance (COI)?

Yes. We carry general liability insurance and can provide a Certificate of Insurance for commercial property managers, HOAs, or any client who requires proof of coverage before a job begins. Request a COI when you call or submit your form.
