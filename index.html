Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers

How do I dispose of appliances in Moore County?

Large appliances (like refrigerators, washers, and dryers) can be dropped off at the Carthage Landfill or designated waste recycling sites. Alternatively, you can save the heavy lifting and loading by contacting us—we'll handle the appliance disconnect, carrying, loading, and ensure it is properly recycled or disposed of according to local environmental regulations.

Are landfills in Moore County open on Sundays?

No, the Carthage Landfill and all Moore County waste/recycling collection sites are closed on Sundays. They generally operate Monday through Saturday. If you have junk that needs to go immediately on the weekend, we offer scheduling flexibility, including Saturday service and prompt weekday pickups, to accommodate your schedule.

What is the average cost of junk removal in Pinehurst, NC?

Junk removal pricing in Pinehurst, NC, depends on the volume of items and truck space required. Single-item pickups typically range from $75 to $150, partial loads range from $150 to $350, and full truckloads range from $350 to $600. We offer free, transparent, no-obligation estimates prior to starting any job.

What items are not accepted by local trash services?

Standard household trash services do not accept large bulk items (like hot tubs, pianos, and construction debris), hazardous materials, automotive fluids, batteries, and paint. While we cannot transport hazardous chemicals, we do haul away almost all non-hazardous bulky items, including old furniture, yard debris, appliances, and hot tubs.
