Southern Pines Junk Removal: A City With Layered Needs

Disposal Routing for Southern Pines Junk Removal Jobs

Every item that leaves a Southern Pines property is routed deliberately. Here's where things go:

  • Usable furniture and household goods: Aberdeen Habitat for Humanity ReStore (1290 Sandhills Blvd, Aberdeen — about 5 miles from downtown Southern Pines). Open Tue–Sat 10AM–4PM. Donation receipts on request.
  • Clothing and smaller household items: Salvation Army Southern Pines or local church donation programs throughout Moore County.
  • Refrigerant appliances (fridges, freezers, window AC): Certified appliance recyclers with EPA Section 608 compliance. Cannot go directly to the landfill.
  • Electronics (TVs, computers, monitors): NC-registered e-waste facilities. Banned from NC landfills under the Electronics Recycling Responsibility Act.
  • Scrap metal: Local metal recyclers. Offsets disposal costs where possible.
  • Everything else: Moore County Solid Waste Facility, 220 Landfill Road, Carthage. Tipping fees included in your quoted price.
